Privacy Policy

MathTutor by Insighteck · Last updated February 27, 2026

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

MathTutor is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable child privacy regulations. We take your family's privacy seriously.

Information We Do NOT Collect

MathTutor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from children or parents. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not contain links to external websites accessible by children
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Stored on Device

All app data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server. This includes:

  • Student profiles — names and avatar settings chosen by the user
  • Learning progress — quiz scores, game results, skill mastery levels
  • Achievements and rewards — stickers earned, XP, and daily goal progress
  • App preferences — voice settings, pencil settings, and other configuration

This data stays on your device and can be deleted at any time from the Parent Portal settings.

Camera and Photo Library

MathTutor may request access to your device camera for two optional features:

  • Worksheet scanning — to photograph completed paper worksheets for automatic grading
  • Avatar photo — to set a custom face photo for the child's avatar

Photos are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded or shared. Camera access can be revoked at any time in your device Settings.

Subscriptions

MathTutor offers optional in-app subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not process or have access to any payment information. Subscription management, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple. See Apple's subscription support page for details.

Parental Controls

All parent-facing features (settings, reports, data management) are protected behind a parent gate requiring an adult action to access. Children cannot modify account settings, manage subscriptions, or access external links.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time:

  • Individual student profiles can be removed from the Parent Portal
  • All data is erased when the app is uninstalled from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
