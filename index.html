Frequently Asked Questions

What ages is MathTutor designed for?

MathTutor covers Kindergarten through Grade 5, suitable for children ages 4 to 11. Content is aligned to NY Next Generation Mathematics Learning Standards.

Does MathTutor work offline?

Yes. MathTutor is fully offline-capable. All lessons, games, and progress tracking work without an internet connection.

Can multiple children use the same device?

Yes. You can create multiple student profiles from the Parent Portal. Each child gets their own progress, achievements, and avatar.

How does adaptive learning work?

MathTutor tracks your child's accuracy and speed across 60+ skills and adjusts difficulty in real time. It targets the Zone of Proximal Development to keep your child challenged without frustration.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > your name > Subscriptions on your device to view, change, or cancel your plan.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open MathTutor on your new device and go to the subscription screen. Tap "Restore Purchases" to reactivate your Premium access using the same Apple ID.

Is MathTutor safe for my child?

Absolutely. MathTutor is 100% COPPA compliant. There are no ads, no third-party tracking, and no external links accessible to children. All parental features are behind a parent gate.
